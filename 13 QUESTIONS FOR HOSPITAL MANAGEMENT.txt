13 QUESTIONS FOR HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT SQL ANALYTICS

Q1) create a table showing the total number of patients, doctors , appointments, treatments and total revenue generated by the hospital.

Q2) Analyze the monthly revenue trend and identify the month with the highest and lowest revenue.

Q3) identify the top 10 patients based on their total spending on treatments and billing.

Q4) find the top 5 doctors who generated the highest revenue for the hospital.

Q5) compare the number of appointments handled by each doctor and rank them from highest to lowest.

Q6) determine which doctor has treated the highest no. of unique patients. 

Q7) identify the most frequently performed treatment and calculate the revenue generated from each treatment type.

Q8) analyze appointment status by calculating the percentage of completed, cancelled and pending appointments.

Q9) find the busiest day and busiest month based on the number of appointments scheduled.

Q10) create a specialization wise table showing the total number of doctors, appointments, patients treated and revenue generated. 

Q11) analyze payment methods by finding the total revenue and percentage contribution of each payment method (cash, upi, card etc.) 

Q12) rank doctors with each specialization based on the revenue they generated using window functions.

Q13) create a table showing total number bills, total revenue and average bill amount.
